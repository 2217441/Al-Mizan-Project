# Project Manager Runbooks

## 1. Project Planning
**Trigger:** New project initiation or major phase start.
**Steps:**
1.  Define objectives and success criteria.
2.  Identify stakeholders and their expectations.
3.  Create Work Breakdown Structure (WBS).
4.  Estimate resources and timelines.
5.  Develop risk management plan.

## 2. Status Reporting
**Trigger:** Weekly or per-sprint cycle.
**Steps:**
1.  Gather progress updates from all agents/teams.
2.  Compare actuals vs. baseline (timeline, budget, scope).
3.  Identify blockers and risks.
4.  Generate status report for stakeholders.

## 3. Risk Management
**Trigger:** Identification of a new risk or issue.
**Steps:**
1.  Log the risk in the Risk Register.
2.  Assess probability and impact.
3.  Develop mitigation strategy.
4.  Assign owner and due date.
5.  Monitor until resolved.
