# UX Research Plan: Al-Mizan

> **Author**: UX Researcher Agent
> **Date**: 2026-01-28
> **SFIA Alignment**: URCH Level 5 (Determines research approach)

---

## 1. Research Objectives
To validate the "Traceable Truth" value proposition with two core segments:
1.  **Seekers**: General users seeking Islamic knowledge.
2.  **Scholars**: Experts verifying the graph integrity.

## 2. Methodology
### Qualitative (Discovery)
*   **In-Depth Interviews**: 1hr sessions with Scholars to understand their verification workflow.
*   **Contextual Inquiry**: Observing Students using current tools (Google, Sunnah.com).

### Quantitative (Validation)
*   **First-Click Testing**: Validating the "Tree of Truth" navigation structure.
*   **Tree Testing**: Testing the taxonomy of the Knowledge Graph.

## 3. Recruitment Strategy
*   **Scholars**: Outreach via IIUM Faculty (internal pilot).
*   **Seekers**: Snowball sampling from MSA (Muslim Student Association) networks.

## 4. Synthesis & Reporting
*   **Artifacts**: Journey Maps, Persona Archetypes (" The Diligent Verifier").
*   **Cadence**: Bi-weekly "Insight Drops" to Product & Design teams.
